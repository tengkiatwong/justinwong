About Me

Web Development started out being a boredo trip to the community library. I picked out a book at random: Ruby on Rails - Michael Hartl, knowing nothing about coding, I dived right into it, just because hacking seemed so cool in the movies. My ambitious self gave me two weeks to finish that book, that two weeks turned into two months, and that, turned into two years. Then here I am, a web developer and computing undergraduate.

While my expertise centers around design and development, my work with various startups in Singapore has taken me on an exciting journey from front to back, back to front and everywhere else in between. I go where I am needed, always more than happy to learn a new framework or library, because I believe there is always the right tool for the job.
