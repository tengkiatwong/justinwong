About Me

My journey in Web Development began with a regular trip to my community library. Wanting to learn a new skill, I browsed through the library catalogue and the book "Ruby on Rails" by Michael Hartl caught my eye. At that time, I had no experience with coding, but the idea of creating my own website enthralled me. Determined and resolute, I decided to borrow that book and plunge myself into the exciting world of Web Development.

Since then, I have come a long way to be where I am today -- a web-developer and undergraduate in Information Systems. I have helped startups gather more than $250,000 in seed funding with my work, and I am looking to do even more. While my expertise centers around design and development, my work with various startups in Singapore has taken me on an exciting journey from front-end to the back-end and everywhere else in between. I go where I am needed, always more than happy to learn a new framework or library, because I believe there is always the right tool for the job. Nevertheless, I am excited for the exhilarating adventure that lies ahead of me as I stand in the sweet spot where beautiful design and clean development collides.

The best part of the job is working with like-minded people
